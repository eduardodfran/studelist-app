Features

Discover the wide range of functionalities that Studelist offers to enhance your learning experience and streamline your academic life.

Notes

Create, organize, and manage your notes efficiently. Our intuitive note-taking feature allows you to categorize your notes, set reminders, and even collaborate with peers to ensure you never miss an important detail.

To-Do List

Organize your tasks with a highly customizable to-do list. Prioritize your tasks, set deadlines, and receive notifications to stay on top of your assignments and responsibilities effortlessly.

Events

Keep track of all your important events with our comprehensive event management tool. Schedule events, set reminders, and sync them with your calendar to ensure you never miss out on important dates and deadlines.

Pomodoro Timer

Boost your productivity with our built-in Pomodoro timer. This scientifically proven technique helps you manage your time effectively by breaking your work into intervals, enhancing focus, and reducing burnout.
